Game Rules

  • The goal is to get the total value of your hand as close to 21 as possible, without going over.
  • If at the end of each round, the total of your hand is higher than the total of the dealer's hand without having gone over 21, you win the round.
  • If the total of your hand goes above 21, you bust and lose that round.
  • If the total of the dealer's hand goes above 21, you win the round.
  • The player's turn will occur first, followed by the dealer.
  • The player must select an amount to wager before the round begins.
  • After the initial cards are shown, the player has a chance to double down. By doubling down, the player's wager amount for that round is doubled, and they draw exactly one additional card.
  • The dealer is required to hit when the dealer's hand total 16 or less.
